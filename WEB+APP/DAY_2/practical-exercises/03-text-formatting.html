Text-Formatting Tags

strong: Marks text with strong importance — this part really matters (usually shown bold).

b: Draws attention to text without extra importance — this is stylistically bold only.

em: Adds emphasis that changes meaning — I really mean it (usually shown italic).

i: Italic for an alternate voice or term, such as a foreign phrase — bona fide.

mark: Highlights text for reference — this part is highlighted.

small: Shows smaller side comments, like fine print — terms and conditions apply.

sub: Subscript, sitting below the baseline — water is H2O.

sup: Superscript, sitting above the baseline — E = mc2.
