Flexbox is designed for laying out items in a 
single direction—either a single row or a single column. 
It gives container components the magical ability to expand 
or shrink their child items to fill available space dynamically 
without overflowing.


display: flex;


Unlike Flexbox, 
CSS Grid is built for handling 
rows and columns simultaneously. 
It allows you to design rigid, 
complex chessboards or newspaper-style grid systems 
that adjust perfectly across screen transitions.
